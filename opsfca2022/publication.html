Dear Authors,

OPSFCA-2022 has different ways to publish the papers presented during OPSFCA-2022. Please find the related details as follows.

1. ABSTRACT PROCEEDING BOOK [Include in Registration Fee]

  • If the Authors submit ABSTRACT TEXTS, then, after getting referees evaluations for these abstracts, they will be published in ABSTRACT PROCEEDING BOOK of OPSFCA-2022 with ISBN number before 1 October 2022.
  • For FULL-TEXT PAPERS, Authors have to submit their FULL-TEXT PAPERS online via the submission system of OPSFCA-2022 until 25 November 2022. These FULL-TEXT PAPERS will be published in the FULL-TEXT PROCEEDING BOOK of OPSFCA-2022 after getting at least two positive reports.
2. FULL-TEXT PROCEEDING BOOK [Include in Registration Fee]
  • In the beginning, if the Authors submit FULL-TEXT PAPERS, then, after getting at least two positive referee reports, FULL-TEXT PAPERS will be published in FULL-TEXT PROCEEDING BOOK of OPSFCA-2022 with ISBN number.
3. Dolomites Research Notes on Approximation [Indexed by SCOPUS, Q3), Scopus CiteScore: 1.8, Include in Registration Fee]
  • https://www.emis.de/journals/DRNA/index-4.html
  • Only selected papers (those areas related to the special issue) will be considered in this issue. Therefore, if they want to submit their FULL-TEXT PAPERS to this special issue, they need to contact Prof. Praveen Agarwal after the conference.
4. SPRINGER PROCEEDING BOOK [Springer, Free, Proposal Submitted]
  • Detailed information will be communicated as soon as possible.
